Student Dropout Prediction System

The Student Dropout Prediction System is a Machine Learning-powered web application developed to predict whether a student is likely to Graduate, remain Enrolled, or become a Dropout. The prediction model analyzes student demographic, academic, and institutional information to assist schools in identifying students who require early academic intervention.

System Features

Machine Learning

Uses a trained Random Forest model to classify students as Graduate, Enrolled, or Dropout with high prediction accuracy.

Early Intervention

Identifies students who may require academic assistance before they become at risk of dropping out.

Prediction History

Automatically stores every prediction for monitoring, evaluation, and reporting purposes.
